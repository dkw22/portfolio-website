About me

This is me.

I’m a full-stack software engineer freelancing. I take pride in ensuring my applications are pixel-perfect, and I especially enjoy turning ideas into reality. Prior to engineering, I was a Digital Marketing Specialist the digital marketing team at Hyper Finds. I was responsible for a multitude of clients marketing campaigns, email management, and data analysis of clients accounts. I’m motivated by product design and real-world impact.

In my spare time, I enjoy going on long rides that lead to a surprise and listening to music. For example, a fun JS application I made used the Spotify API to play music when different pictures are clicked; one of my favorite projects to date! I believe in companies that encourage life outside of work, understand that thorough planning can save hours of programming, appreciate engineers with diverse backgrounds and skillsets, and value getting sh*t done.

There is nothing I love more than checking things off a list, figuring out how to turn an idea into reality, and creating order out of chaos. I’m not afraid to work hard, and I’m passionate about delivering results.
